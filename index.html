Soundify: Generating Audio from Video using Latent Diffusion Models

Paper in preparation, coming soon...

Abstract

With the surge in the field of text-to-video generation, the application value of generating audio directly from silent videos has increased. We introduce Soundify, an audio synthesis method based on a Latent Diffusion Model (LDM). We employ Contrastive Audio-Visual Pretraining (CAVP) to learn more temporally and semantically aligned features. In the diffusion model, we utilize prior information similar to ControlNet, which enhances the temporal consistency between audio and video. Additionally, we incorporate text modality information to improve the sound generation capability in static scenes.
